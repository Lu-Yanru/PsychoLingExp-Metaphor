Instructions

In this experiment, you will read some sentences containing metaphors. Please read the sentences carefully and try to understand their meaning as good as possible. Please read the sentences only once.

You can press the spacebar to proceed to the next sentence.

After reading the sentences, you will see a string of letters.

Your task is to decide whether or not the letters form a word in the English language as quickly and accurately as possible.

Press the F key to answer No, or the J key to answer Yes.

Please keep your left finger on the F-key, and your right finger on the J-key, as well as you thumbs on the spacebar throughout the experiment.

After each of the metaphors, you will see a waiting screen. You do not need to press spacebar to continue to the next metaphor, it will be presented automatically after a moment.

First you will be presented with two practice sentences.

After the practice sentences, a waiting screen will prompt you to press the spacebar to start the experiment.
